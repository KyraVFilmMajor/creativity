Creative Leader Qualities

  1. Can make more business model changes to realize their strategies.
  2. They invite more disruptive innovation.
  3. They are comfortable with ambiguity.
  4. They score higher when it comes to innovation.
  5. They are known to be courageous and visionary people, enough to make decisions that affect and alter the status quo.
  6. They will consider un-heard of ways to drastically change the way things are run around their company.

Injecting creativity into the business world infinite endeavors such as idea brainstorming, innovation labs, and flexible time for employees to enjoy fun activities would not only help the company thrive but also improve the mental health and well-being of team members.
