How To Be Creative

  1. Give into what your mind is trying to convey to you/tell you.
  2. Let yourself find your creativity but don't try to force it.
  3. Be Audacious, own whatever you're creating and be confident in each decision you make with it.
  4. Let the ideas flow through you.
  5. Let yourself be imperfect, there's no such thing as perfect. It's a weird human concept. Being imperfect brings out more originality from you.
  6. Do not go back on any decisions you make and do not question yourself.
  7. Do not second guess each decision you have before you make it, it'll only cause you more stress.
